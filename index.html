    • The chosen idea

    After we have talked with Mr. Chiu, we decided to work on AIS uniform policy website because it had clear audiences and client.

    Meeting with the client

    We met with Mr. Hardistry during the class, and we have found that Mr. Hardisty did not want the background to blend with the things we put on the website

    Also, he wanted let people to easily navigate through the website. He said the reason why he wanted this website is he wants to save his time of explaining the rules and policy of the AIS uniforms

    Audience

    After we met Mr. hardisty, Mr. Hardisty wanted us to make it clear that our website is particularly for AIS High School students.

    Objectives

      Objectives of the website are
  • To let Mr. Hardisty save time by not explaining policies to students
  • To let students understand the uniform policy without asking teachers
  • To let students have access to uniform policy 24/7
  • To show AIS students that AIS has no tolerance of wrong uniform dress code

    • Success Criteria

    To let students know about uniform policy by clicking less than 3 times   
    Let the audience know the uniform dos and donts  
    Use 2 or less visual buttons on home page  
    Use Javascript to create a minime or website menu bar
